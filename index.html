FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before getting started.

When we match you with a developer, DevLink takes 15% of the agreed project fee. This covers finding, vetting, and connecting you with the right person. The developer receives the remaining 85% directly. There are no hidden fees beyond this.
Every developer in our roster has been personally reviewed before being accepted. We check their previous work, experience level, and communication. We only work with developers we'd confidently recommend ourselves.
We recommend agreeing on a clear brief and milestones before work begins. If something isn't right, get in touch and we'll help mediate. In cases where a project genuinely falls through, we'll do our best to find you an alternative.
Yes — if you've worked with someone from our roster before and want to work with them again, just mention it in your enquiry. We'll do our best to accommodate this, subject to their availability.
It depends on scope. Small features or UI work can take a few days, while full game builds may take several weeks. We'll give you a realistic estimate when we review your brief, and match you with developers whose availability fits your deadline.
We accept developers at different experience levels, but everyone goes through the same review process. Clients are always informed of a developer's experience level before being matched, so there are no surprises.
